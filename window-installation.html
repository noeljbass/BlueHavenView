Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful answers before you request a quote.

Why does professional installation matter?

Installation affects air infiltration, water management, operation, appearance, and the lifespan of your replacement windows.

Will installation be messy?

Window projects involve removal and installation work, but we plan for a clean, respectful process and leave the work areas orderly.

Do you measure before ordering?

Yes. Proper measurement is an important part of achieving fit, performance, and a finished look.

Can you install different styles in one home?

Yes. Many homes combine double hung windows with picture, casement, geometric, bay, bow, garden, or patio door options.

Do installers explain how to use the windows?

We can review basic operation, care, and maintenance considerations before wrapping up the project.
