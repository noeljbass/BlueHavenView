# Blue Haven Windows Launch SEO Plan

This site is pre-launch, so there is no Search Console query, impression, click, or conversion data yet. The launch plan should use keyword planner data, competitor observations, and the current site architecture as the initial baseline. After launch, replace assumptions with Search Console, Google Business Profile, analytics, and call/form tracking data.

## Primary Launch Priorities

1. **Conversion-first local pages**
   - Keep the homepage, service pages, service-area pages, and guide pages focused on phone calls and estimate requests.
   - Primary CTAs should stay consistent: `Get Free Estimate`, `Call (615) 987-0593`, and `Download Buyer's Guide`.
   - Because appointment booking is not the main flow, use call/contact-form language rather than calendar-booking language.

2. **Core commercial keyword targets**
   - `window replacement`
   - `window replacement nashville`
   - `home window replacement`
   - `window installation`
   - `residential window replacement`
   - `affordable window replacement`
   - `new windows for home`
   - `window replacement company`
   - `local window replacement`
   - `window replacement company near me`
   - `local window company`
   - `replacement window installers near me`

3. **High-intent quote / lead keywords**
   - `window replacement quote`
   - `window quote`
   - `window installation quote`
   - `free window quote`
   - `window consultation`
   - `window replacement offers`
   - These keywords are expensive in Keyword Planner, so they should be paired with strong trust copy, reviews, and low-friction forms.

4. **Supporting service and informational keywords**

## Competitor Observations

The competitor sites support a few launch lessons:

- **West Shore Home** (`westshorehome.com`) emphasizes strong above-the-fold forms, promotions, financing/payment language, free consultation CTAs, large review counts, and trust badges.
- **Efficiency Home Improvements** (`efficiencyhomeimprovements.com`) uses Middle Tennessee positioning and specifically mentions Franklin, Brentwood, Spring Hill, and Greater Nashville.
- **Heritage Replacement Windows** (`heritagereplacementwindows.com`) focuses on Nashville window replacement and “trusted local” positioning.
- **Forst Builders** (`forstbuilders.com`) has a deep Franklin/Brentwood window replacement footprint with service-area pages, window-type pages, gallery/project references, reviews, local business details, and long local SEO copy.

Blue Haven should not try to sound like a large national company. The stronger positioning is: local, family-owned, Christian mission focused, honest communication, Stephen-led service, fair pricing, lifetime warranty options, and a simpler lower-pressure process.

## Launch Measurement Checklist

Before launch or immediately after launch:

- Set up Google Search Console.
- Submit the sitemap once Phase 5 generates it.
- Confirm Google Business Profile URL, phone, service areas, and website URL.
- Add GA4 or another analytics platform.
- Track contact form submissions.
- Track click-to-call events for `tel:+16159870593`.
- Track Buyer’s Guide form submissions.
- Track clicks to `https://sendmestars.com/bluehavenview`.
- Review indexed pages after launch and inspect the service-area and guide hubs first.

## First 30 Days After Launch

1. Confirm all core pages are indexed.
2. Check Search Console for impressions on window replacement and city keywords.
3. Watch for unexpected query opportunities, especially `near me`, city modifiers, quote terms, and guide topics.
4. Add internal links from pages receiving impressions to pages that need stronger rankings.
5. Replace placeholders with actual before/after or finished-project images as soon as they are available.
6. Add new reviews to homepage and service pages as they come in.
7. Keep service-area pages truthful; avoid claiming specific projects in a city until examples exist.
